Hobbies

  • Cooking:

    • Cooking is the process of preparing food using heat, transforming raw ingredients into edible meals. It involves various techniques like baking, frying, boiling, and grilling, according to Britannica. This process enhances food's palatability, digestibility, and nutritional value, making it safer and easier to consume.

  • Travelling:

    • A journey is a trip, especially one to a faraway place that takes place over a long period of time. The word trip can refer to any instance of traveling from one place to another, no matter how long or short it is.

  • Talking With Friends:

    • Talking with friends involves engaging in conversations that range from casual small talk to in-depth discussions on various topics, often shared experiences, interests, and personal reflections. It can be a source of laughter, support, and emotional bonding.
