Flipkart is a leading Indian e-commerce marketplace, founded in 2007 by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal. It started as an online bookstore and has grown to offer millions of products across various categories, including electronics, fashion, and groceries. Flipkart Corporate Website has a massive customer base and a significant number of sellers.
